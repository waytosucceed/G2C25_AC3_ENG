What did we learn today?

An adjective is a word that describes a noun (person, place, or thing) and a pronoun.
It tells us more about what something looks like, feels like, or how it acts.

Here's a fun way to remember adjectives:
A - Add details
D - Describe things
J - Jazz up your sentences
E - Excite your readers
C - Create clear pictures with words
T - Tell more about nouns
I - Improve your writing
V - Vivid language
E - Engage your audience

Example: The tall tree is in the park.
Noun: Tree
Adjective: Tall
The word "tall" is an adjective. It tells us how high the tree is.
